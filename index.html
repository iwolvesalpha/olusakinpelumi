About Me

I'm Korede, a passionate web developer, brand manager, digital marketer/ strategist, photograper and project manager from Lagos, Nigeria. I'm driven by my love for technology and creativity, and I've always been fascinated by the limitless possibilities of the digital world. As a lifelong football fan, I'm a proud supporter of Manchester United, even though the team might be going through a rough patch. I'm also a car enthusiast, admiring the craftsmanship of Audi and Ford, and I'm impressed by the innovative advancements Tesla is making.

Besides my professional work, I'm actively involved in my church community, where I contribute to the media department. When I have free time, I enjoy exploring philosophical works like Aristotle's writings and immersing myself in captivating TV shows. Peaky Blinders is one of my all-time favorites, as I see a lot of myself in Tommy Shelby's character. Recently, I've added Game of Thrones, Ozark, and Breaking Bad to my list of top shows. These series offer compelling storylines, exceptional characters, and a range of emotions that keep me hooked.
